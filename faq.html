Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?

A: Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your payment.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept EFT, PayPal, PayFast, and PayFlex (if available in your region).

Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive tracking information via email.

Q: Do you offer dropshipping?

A: Yes, we provide dropshipping options with fast delivery for all products.

Q: Can I return a product?

A: Yes, please see our return policy in the terms & conditions section. Returns must be within 7 days of delivery.

Q: How do I leave a review?

A: Click on the product, scroll down to the review section, write your review, and submit it.

Q: Can I ask questions about a product?

A: Yes, we have a dedicated page for customer questions where you can ask anything about our products.

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

Q: Is it safe to pay online?

A: Absolutely! We use secure payment methods to ensure your information is protected.

Q: Do you offer discounts?

A: Yes, check our “Deals of the Day” banner for the latest offers and discounts.
